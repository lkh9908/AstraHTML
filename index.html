VISION & VALUE

Community Leads
Community leads always. The community is both the baby in a cradle and the mother sitting by. If we ever ignore the power of the community, it would be either a mother losing her loved baby or a baby losing protection from its mother. So, having the community in the 1st place is more than necessary, but vital for the long-term prosperity of the brand.


Time & Space
It's easy to make one-time popularity, but only through the examination of time can a real treasure be created. What we're doing is creating the beautiful and meaningful to pass it on, long and wide.


Start The Engine
The decentralized world is still in its infancy. It's both a chance and a challenge. While we keep creating, exploring, and experimenting, setbacks and unexpected stuff happen. Instead of pausing and looking, we believe starting the engine will always head us to somewhere surprising.
